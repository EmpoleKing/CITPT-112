Learn about the various card types in Magic: The Gathering!

Creature Cards

Creature cards are that can attack and block. Creatures have power (damage they deal) and toughness (damage they can take before being destroyed). They are the primary combat units in the game.

Sorcery Cards

These are spells that create a one-time effect and then go to the 'graveyard'. Sorceries can only be cast during your main phases when you have priority.

Enchantment Cards

Enchantment cards are persistent effects that remain on the battlefield until they are removed. Enchantments can affect the game in various ways, such as boosting your creatures or hindering your opponent's actions.

Artifact Cards

Artifact cards are magical items and constructs. Artifacts can have various abilities and can be used by any player. They are permanents and stay on the battlefield until removed.

Land Cards

Lands represent the various terrains and locations in the game. Lands are used to generate mana, which is necessary to cast spells and summon creatures. Each land can be tapped to produce mana of a specific color.

Planeswalker Cards

Planeswalkers are powerful allies that can be summoned to aid you in battle. They have loyalty counters that can be used to activate their abilities. Planeswalkers can be attacked by creatures and lose loyalty counters when they take damage.
